About Us

We are the world's largest university

The teaching is done through lectures and practical classes (where students do experiments in science subjects) organized by the Department. The colleges also organize supervisions. These are small lessons in groups of 1 to 4: the students do some homework and then talk about it with a teacher. This is often considered to be one of the best things about studying at United Nations University. The teaching is very personal and students have the chance to ask lots of questions and really understand the subject.
